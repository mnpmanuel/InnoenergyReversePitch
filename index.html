ABOUT

REVERSE PITCH is an initiative where companies present their Energy and Clean Tech-related challenges to an audience of Entrepreneurs with strong expertise in technology and knowledge in the Energy and Clean Tech field.

HOW

The ultimate goal is to solve problems, encourage innovation, create value, and generate business opportunities in an exclusive event.

  • 1st

    Companies present their challenge (pitch) to an audience of startups followed by a networking cocktail.

  • 2nd

    Startups have access to additional information (Q &A) and have 1 month to submit an initial proposal for the company.

  • 3rd

    If the company has interest, the startup can be hired or the project could be submitted for funding by InnoEnergy under the Highway or Innovation Projects programs.

  • 4th

    Projects may have their development funded by InnoEnergy as an Innovation Project.

ORGANIZATIONS

InnoEnergy is an European company dedicated to promoting innovation, entrepreneurship and education in the field of Energy Efficiency and Sustainable Energy Industry. Its main goal is to create a positive impact on energy sustainability in Europe, by changing the perceptions of people and by the creation of innovative products and services that encourage the emergence of successful companies.
Through its various services, InnoEnergy supports academics, entrepreneurs, and Institutes/centers of research that have the capacity and motivation to create value in the area of sustainable energy.

imatch is an innovation consulting company that works in the areas of marketing, entrepreneurship, sales and organizational culture.
