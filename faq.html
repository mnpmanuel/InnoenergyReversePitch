F.A.Q.

Is this just another contest?

No, this is a lead generation and business development event. The goal isn't to earn a prize at the end of the competition, but rather to establish new and profitable business relationships. The event aims to put together carefully selected innovative solutions to solve real challenges in the market.

What if the agreement between company and supplier does not happen?

The agreement between the company and the startup shall not occur if the parties fail to reach an agreement.

What happens after the event?

After the event, the negotiation process starts from those startups that presented the best case and were selected by the companies. It is up to the startups to lead this after event process. The InnoEnergy Reverse Pitch team can support the connection at this stage and in case the agreement considers an Innovation Project.

How is the funding application done?

After an agreement between the company and startup, as well as the definition of the solution to be developed, if funding is required, the company should contact InnoEnergy to follow the funding program procedures.

What if I have any additional questions?

You can reach us by sending an e-mail. All your questions will be answered shortly.

Registration & Conditions

  • Projects may have their development funded by InnoEnergy as an Innovation Project.
  • The companies have no obligation to contract any service or product from startups with whom they have engaged.
  • Companies should be willing to give a testimonial that will be used to communicate the event.
